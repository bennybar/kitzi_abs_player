Kitzi is a modern audiobook client for Audiobookshelf servers. Stream and download your audiobooks with a beautiful, intuitive interface.

Features:
• Stream audiobooks from your Audiobookshelf server
• Download audiobooks for offline listening
• Background playback with lock screen controls
• Sleep timer functionality
• Playback speed control
• Chapter navigation
• Download management
• Dark theme support
• Android Auto support

Perfect for audiobook enthusiasts who want a clean, feature-rich listening experience.
