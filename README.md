Transform your audiobook listening experience with Kitzi ABS Player, the most comprehensive client for Audiobookshelf servers. Whether you're commuting, exercising, or relaxing at home, enjoy seamless access to your entire audiobook library.

✨ KEY FEATURES:

📚 Complete Audiobookshelf Integration
• Connect to your personal Audiobookshelf server
• Access your entire audiobook and podcast library
• Sync reading progress across all devices
• Support for multiple user accounts

🎵 Advanced Audio Playback
• High-quality audio streaming and offline playback
• Background playback with lock screen controls
• Android Auto support for hands-free listening
• Customizable playback speed (0.5x to 3x)
• Sleep timer and chapter navigation

Smart Download Management
• Download audiobooks for offline listening
• Intelligent download queue management
• WiFi-only download option to save data
• Progress tracking and resume capabilities

🎨 Intuitive User Interface
• Clean, modern design optimized for audiobooks
• Easy library browsing and search
• Bookmark and progress tracking
• Dark mode support

🔒 Privacy & Security
• Direct connection to your server - no data collection
• Secure authentication
• Local storage for offline content

Perfect for audiobook enthusiasts who want full control over their listening experience. Kitzi ABS Player brings the power of Audiobookshelf to your Android device with professional-grade features and reliability.
